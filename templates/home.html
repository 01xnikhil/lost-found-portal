ABOUT OUR LOST & FOUND PORTAL

Our Lost & Found Portal is a user-friendly web application designed to help people reconnect with their lost belongings in a quick and efficient way. 🚀 Users can easily report lost items by providing important details such as description, location, date, and images, while others can post items they have found, creating a shared platform for recovery. 📦 The system allows users to browse available items and search based on categories, keywords, or locations, making the process simple and organized.
